How It All Started

AQ Food, a wholly-owned subsidiary of AQ Capital, was founded in 2019 as a privately owned cattle company. It currently manages a 300 acre farm in Gua Musang; a district located in Kelantan, Malaysia.

Quality Beef

Our farm is home to 5000 selected cattle from Thailand and Malaysia, producing Halal quality beef and dairy products along the way.

Our Machinery

Our company is commited in providing quality output across our vast land by ensuring our machinery are well-maintained.
